Many fatal accidents in hazardous environments happen due to human error, and most can be prevented with appropriate information and models.

SoterAI automatically creates a Digital Twin of the environment in real time.
A Digital Twin is a digital representation of the environment used to run simulations to enhance human decision-making.

Safety analytics

Our expertise lies in modeling, predicting and evaluating safety.

We have created unique safety and risk metrics, prediction models and simulation tools that allow the identification and avoidance of unsafe scenarios in real-time.

Detailed safety reports, scores and dashboards are also generated for the provided each scenario to be analized.

Virtually any hazardous environment that involves machines or humans can be analized. Various data formats are supported: video files, GPS/IMU, LIDAR, and more.
